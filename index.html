The Carpathian Montains

The Carpathians Mountains are majestic mountains located in the western part of Ukraine.Their beauty is unsurpassed: green forests, mountain streams, pristine lakes and picturesque panoramas.

This region is known for its variety of natural attractions, including high peaks, waterfalls and caves.

The Ukrainian Carpathians offer many opportunities for active recreation: hiking, cycling routes, ski trails and excursions. Here you can enjoy the hospitality of local residents, taste delicious traditional cuisine and get to know Ukrainian culture.

The Carpathians are a magical place where unique nature and open hearts of people meet.
